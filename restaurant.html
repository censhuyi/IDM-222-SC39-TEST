Restaurant Design

Location: Philadelphia

Concept Statement

The design concept for the restaurant is a biophilic design style. This Japanese cuisine restaurant combines natural materials, textures, and organic shapes such as wood, fiber, and concrete, conveying the idea of getting people closer to nature in today’s fast-paced life. The Greenery is incorporated throughout the interior space to express the relaxing and easygoing moment.
